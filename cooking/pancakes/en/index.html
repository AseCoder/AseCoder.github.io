American style pancakes

In finnish

Batter size

For a couple of people


Family size

Ingredients

Amount Ingredient
egg
milk
wheat flour
baking powder
sugar
vanilla sugar
salt
liquid margarine

Instructions

  1. Break egg in a bowl
  2. Mix thoroughly
  3. Add of milk
  4. Add of wheat flour
  5. Add of baking powder
  6. Add of sugar
  7. Add of vanilla sugar
  8. Add of salt
  9. Mix
  10. Add of margarine
  11. Mix thoroughly
  12. Let rise in the fridge
